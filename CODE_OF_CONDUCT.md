# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
in full. The full text — including the standards, enforcement
responsibilities, scope, enforcement guidelines, and attribution — lives at
the upstream URL and is considered part of this repository by reference.

## Our pledge (summary)

We — contributors and maintainers of `ddqn-router` — pledge to make
participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone,
and to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces (issues, pull
requests, discussions, chat) and also applies when an individual is
officially representing the community in public spaces.

## Reporting and enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainer at:

> `kirillmozolev35@gmail.com`

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The
maintainer is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter
of any incident.

Enforcement follows the Contributor Covenant
[Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines):
correction → warning → temporary ban → permanent ban, applied as proportionate
to the behavior observed.
